About

Wajeeha Nasar is a project engineer at Hexagon Purus Maritime. Perviously she served as a scientific research at the Department of ICT and Natural Sciecnes, Norwegian University of Science and Technology, Ålesund. She has a doctoral dergee in Computer Science, and has successfully defended her PhD degree in May, 2024 with the title "Artificial Intellgence-Informed Decision Support for Search and Rescue. She hold two masters degrees, one is masters in simulation and visualiztion, NTNU i Ålesund, Norway, and second is masters in electrical engineering, Institute of Space and Technology, Islamabad, Pakistan. She completed her undergraduation degree as electronics engineer from The Islamia University of Bahawalpur, Pakistan. Moreover, she worked as a university lecturer and teachning assistant in several courses at the Department of ICT and Natural Sciecnes, NTNU i Ålesund. She is a skilled researcher involved in several EU projects such as United for Smart and Sustainable Cities (U4SSC), NorDark, Twinfjord for smart city planning. She is a passionate researcher and system engineer specializing in human-centered design, user-centric research, and programming in software development, with a keen eye for understanding user/stakeholders needs. She emphasize on involving and collaborating with stakeholders from the very begining of any project to understand the ground realities and address their problems through developing demonstrators that fulfilled their needs. She aspires to contribute her expertise in system design and development, human-centered design, sustainable development,user-centric research, and programming.
